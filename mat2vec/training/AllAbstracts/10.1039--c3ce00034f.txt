A series of coordination polymers based on 3,5-dinitrosalicylic acid (H<inf>2</inf>sal) and nine flexible bis(triazole) ligands, namely, [Cu <inf>4</inf>(sal)<inf>4</inf>(L1)<inf>2</inf>(H<inf>2</inf>O)]·2H <inf>2</inf>O (1), [Cu<inf>4</inf>(sal)<inf>4</inf>(L2)<inf>2</inf>(H <inf>2</inf>O)] (2), [Cu<inf>2</inf>(sal)<inf>2</inf>(L3)(H<inf>2</inf>O)] (3), [Cu<inf>2</inf>(sal)<inf>2</inf>(L4)(H<inf>2</inf>O)] (4), [Cu <inf>5</inf>(sal)<inf>4</inf>(OH)<inf>2</inf>(L5)<inf>2</inf>(H <inf>2</inf>O)<inf>3</inf>]·H<inf>2</inf>O (5), [Cu(sal)(L6)] (6), [Cu<inf>2</inf>(sal)<inf>2</inf>(L7)] (7), [Cu(sal)(L8)]·0.5H <inf>2</inf>O (8), [Cd<inf>2</inf>(sal)<inf>2</inf>(L1)(H<inf>2</inf>O) <inf>2</inf>] (9), [Cd<inf>2</inf>(sal)<inf>2</inf>(L7)<inf>3</inf>] (10) and [Cd<inf>4</inf>(sal)<inf>4</inf>(L9)<inf>4</inf>] (11), where L1 = 1,2-bis(1,2,4-triazol-1-yl)ethane, L2 = 1,3-bis(1,2,4-triazol-1-yl)propane, L3 = 1,4-bis(1,2,4-triazol-1-yl)butane, L4 = 1,5-bis(1,2,4-triazol-1-yl)pentane, L5 = 1,5-bis(1,3,4-triazol-1-yl)pentane, L6 = 1,6-bis(1,2,4-triazol-1-yl)hexane, L7 = 1,10-bis(1,2,4-triazol-1-yl)decane, L8 = 1,4-bis((1H-1,2,4-triazol-1-yl) methyl)benzene and L9 = 1,1′-(2,2′-oxybis(ethane-2,1-diyl))bis(1H-1, 2,4-triazole), have been synthesized and characterized by single crystal X-ray diffraction, elemental analyses, IR spectra, PXRD spectra and TG analysis. Compounds 1 and 4 possess one-dimensional (1D) double chains. Compounds 2, 3 and 5 show two-dimensional (2D) 4<sup>4</sup> networks. Compound 6 displays a three-dimensional (3D) uninodal eight-connected self-penetrating framework with the 4<sup>20</sup>6<sup>8</sup> topology. Compound 7 reveals a 2D 2-fold interpenetrating 4<sup>4</sup>-sql network. Compounds 8 and 9 display 2D undulated layers. Compound 10 shows a 1D chain. Compound 11 reveals a discrete tetranuclear cluster. The UV-vis spectra and optical energy gap of 1-11 have also been investigated in detail. © The Royal Society of Chemistry 2013.
