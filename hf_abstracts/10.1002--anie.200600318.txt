(Figure Presented) Bringing together: Zirconium and hafnium d0 metallocene complexes catalyze the dehydrocoupling of the stibines MesSbH 2 (Mes = mesityl) and dmpSbH2 (dmp = 2,6-dimesitylphenyl) to Sb4Mes4 and (dmp)Sb=Sb(dmp), respectively, with liberation of H2. Kinetic analysis shows that thermal decomposition of hafnium stibide complexes proceeds through α-hydrogen migration and stibinidene elimination. © 2006 Wiley-VCH Verlag GmbH & Co. KGaA.
